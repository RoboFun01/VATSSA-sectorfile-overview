This eAIP is VATSSA's platform to share a custom Aeronautical Information Publication for Controllers and Pilots.

It will contain relevant information on regional airspace and airports within the VATSSA airspace.

This platform does not replace the official up to date information contained in each of the countries AIPs and other official documentation.

//**The information contained in this platform is for flight simulation purposes only and shall not be used, under any circumstances, for real world aviation.**//
